## Tips for Effective Outreach

### Subject Line Best Practices

- **Use specific benefits:** "Free Nutrition Education Platform for Your School"
- **Create urgency:** "Limited Trial Spots Available"
- **Ask a question:** "Is Your School Ready for Engaging Nutrition Education?"
- **Personalize:** "Wisconsin Food Explorer for [School Name]"

### Email Structure

1. **Opening:** Personalized greeting and brief introduction
2. **Problem:** Identify a challenge (engagement, nutrition education, student motivation)
3. **Solution:** Explain how Wisconsin Food Explorer solves the problem
4. **Benefits:** List key features and advantages
5. **Social Proof:** Share success stories or testimonials
6. **Call to Action:** Clear next step (schedule call, request trial, register for webinar)
7. **Closing:** Professional sign-off with contact information

### Personalization Tips

- Use recipient's name and title
- Reference their school or district specifically
- Mention shared contacts or mutual connections
- Tailor benefits to their specific role (principal, teacher, coordinator)
- Include relevant success stories from similar schools

### Follow-Up Strategy

- **First Email:** Initial outreach with overview
- **Wait 1 Week:** If no response, send follow-up
- **Second Email:** Highlight specific benefits or success stories
- **Wait 1 Week:** If still no response, try different approach
- **Third Contact:** Phone call or different channel
- **After Trial:** Regular check-ins and success story sharing

## Tracking and Analytics

### Email Metrics to Monitor

- **Open Rate:** Percentage of recipients who open the email
- **Click-Through Rate:** Percentage who click links in the email
- **Response Rate:** Percentage who reply or take action
- **Conversion Rate:** Percentage who request trial or register

### Optimization Tips

- **A/B Test Subject Lines:** Try different subject lines with similar audiences
- **Track Links:** Use unique URLs to track which emails drive conversions
- **Monitor Timing:** Send emails at different times to see what works best
- **Segment Audiences:** Tailor messages to different roles (principal, teacher, coordinator)
