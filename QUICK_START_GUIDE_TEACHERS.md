# Wisconsin Nutrition Explorer - Quick Start Guide for Teachers

**Get Your Class Playing in 5 Minutes**

---

## 1. Log In and Create Your Class

1. Go to Wisconsin Nutrition Explorer website
2. Click **Log In** and enter your credentials
3. Click **Teachers** in the top navigation
4. Click **Classes** tab → **Create New Class**
5. Enter your class name (e.g., "3rd Grade Nutrition")
6. **Copy the join code** that appears

**Time: 2 minutes**

---

## 2. Have Students Join

1. Display the join code on your classroom screen or write it on the board
2. Students visit the home page and click **Play as Student**
3. Students enter the join code
4. Students enter their name and select a character (cheese, cranberry, corn, or milk)
5. Students appear in your class roster

**Time: 2 minutes**

---

## 3. Start Your First Game

1. Students are ready to play immediately
2. Each player rolls the dice and moves around Wisconsin's map
3. Answer nutrition questions to earn points
4. First player to reach the finish line wins
5. Scores are automatically saved to the leaderboard

**Time: 1 minute to start**

---

## 4. Recognize Student Achievement (Optional)

After the game, celebrate student success with certificates:

1. Click **Certificates** tab
2. Click **Create Individual Certificate** (or **Batch** for entire class)
3. Enter student name(s) and achievement
4. Click **Print** or **Download as PDF**

## Troubleshooting

**Students can't join?**
- Verify join code is correct
- Check that your school network allows access to the platform

**Game not displaying?**
- Refresh your browser
- Clear browser cache
- Try a different browser

**Need help?**
- Click **Help** button in the platform
- Contact your school IT administrator
