## Account Setup and Management

### Requesting a Trial Account

1. Visit Wisconsin Nutrition Explorer website
2. Click **Request a Trial** or contact your district representative
3. Provide school name, district, and primary contact information
4. Specify number of teachers and students
5. Trial account will be set up within 24 hours

### Creating User Accounts

**For Administrators:**
1. Log in to the admin dashboard
2. Click **Manage Users**
3. Click **Add Administrator**
4. Enter name, email, and role
5. Send invitation email to new administrator

**For Teachers:**
1. Click **Add Teacher**
2. Enter name, email, and school assignment
3. Send invitation email to teacher
4. Teacher completes account setup on first login

**For Students:**
1. Teachers create classes and share join codes with students
2. Students join classes directly using join codes
3. No administrator action needed for student accounts

### User Roles and Permissions

| Role | Responsibilities | Permissions |
|------|-----------------|-------------|
| **Administrator** | System management, user accounts, analytics | Full access to all features and settings |
| **Teacher** | Classroom management, student engagement | Create classes, manage students, generate certificates |
| **Nutrition Staff** | Bulk operations, communications | Bulk certificate generation, email campaigns |
| **Student** | Gameplay, learning | Play games, view leaderboards, read success stories |

## Training and Support

### Recommended Training Approach

**Option 1: Group Training Session (Recommended)**
- Duration: 1 hour
- Audience: All teachers who will use the platform
- Content: Platform overview, game demonstration, certificate generation, AI Assistant
- Hands-on: Teachers play a practice game
- Materials: Printed quick-start guides, video tutorial links

**Option 2: Train-the-Trainer Model**
- Duration: 2 hours for champion teachers, 30 minutes for other teachers
- Process: Train 2–3 champion teachers first, then have them train colleagues
- Benefit: Builds internal expertise and peer support
- Scalability: Works well for large districts

**Option 3: Self-Paced Online Training**
- Duration: 30 minutes per teacher
- Content: Video tutorials, user manual, interactive guides
- Support: Optional office hours for questions
- Flexibility: Teachers complete training on their own schedule

## Troubleshooting and Support

### Common IT Issues

**Issue: Users cannot access the platform**

**Solution:**
1. Verify network connection is working
2. Check that Wisconsin Nutrition Explorer domain is not blocked by firewall
3. Try accessing from a different network (e.g., mobile hotspot) to isolate the issue
4. Contact IT support if issue persists

**Issue: Platform is slow or unresponsive**

**Solution:**
1. Check network bandwidth usage
2. Verify internet speed meets minimum requirements (5 Mbps)
3. Try accessing during off-peak hours
4. Clear browser cache and try again
5. Contact support if issue continues

**Issue: Certificates not printing correctly**

**Solution:**
1. Verify printer is connected and has paper
2. Test printing from a different device
3. Try "Print to PDF" as alternative
4. Check browser print settings (margins, scaling)

**Issue: Email not sending**

**Solution:**
1. Verify email address is correct
2. Check that email is not blocked by school email filter
3. Try sending to a different email address
4. Contact IT support if issue persists
